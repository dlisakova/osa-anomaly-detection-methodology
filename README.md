# OSA Anomaly Detection Methodology

This project is devoted to the development of a methodology for detecting anomalous sales drops in high-frequency retail data based on statistical analysis of time series. The study aims to address the problem of ensuring product availability on shelves (On-Shelf Availability, OSA) and reducing cases of stockouts (Out-of-Stock, OOS) in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector under conditions of masked identifiers and high levels of data noise. The developed approach was tested on a large dataset of hourly sales records from a single store of a mid-tier grocery retail chain, provided by Imredi company.
